Creamy mushroom risotto

Source

âœ¨ Description

Creamy mushroom risotto with a Japanese twist. Makes 3 servings.

âœ¨ Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Arborio rice
  • 2 dates
  • 500ml ramen stock
  • 1 medium/big onion
  • 250g brown mushrooms
  • 1 tsp miso paste
  • 200ml cooking cream
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • EVOO (extra virgin olive oil)
  • pine nuts
  • parmesan or grana padano cheese
  • scamorza cheese
  • oregano
  • bread

âœ¨ Steps

  1. Heat the stock in a saucepan (no need to bring to a boil).
  2. In a separate pan, sautee the onion, mushrooms and dates (cut in tiny cubes) with EVOO, salt, paprika and miso paste.
  3. When the onion is caramelized, add the rice and stir.
  4. Add the stock to the mix and stir every once in a while until it evaporates and the rice is cooked.
  5. Add the cooking cream and pine nuts.
  6. When the desired creaminess is achieved, serve with grated parmesan cheese, cubed scamorza (push it into the rice so the heat melts it), oregano to taste and a drizzle of EVOO, as well as bread on the side (don't forget to use it to "mop up" all the sauce off the plate! ðŸ¤Œ ).
