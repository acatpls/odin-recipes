Chickpea salad with halloumi

Source

✨ Description

Chickpea salad with halloumi. Makes 2 servings.

✨ Ingredients

  • 2 cans of chickpeas (400g each)
  • 250g halloumi cheese
  • 2 tsp harissa paste
  • 1/4 pomegranate
  • 2 dates (cut in cubes)
  • 1/2 bell pepper (pick your favorite color)
  • salt to taste
  • EVOO (extra virgin olive oil)
  • 2-3 cherry tomatoes (e.g. black cherry)
  • 2-3 walnuts
  • (optional: red onion and avocado)

✨ Steps

  1. Airfry the chickpeas until they sound crunchy (note: when you hear one explode, lower the temperature).
  2. Cut the hallomi in squares, season with harissa and pan fry them with a tbsp of EVOO.
  3. Cut all the other ingredients: bell peppers, tomatoes, pomegranate, dates, onion (optional), avocado (optional).
  4. Once the chickpeas and halloumi are ready, mix everything, add salt, drizzle some EVOO and crumble the walnuts on top.
